About Me

I am a 33 year old native of Chicago that has spent the last few years working and living in Afghanistan.

As an innovative and results-driven Project Manager, I have managed projects on empowering Afghan women and helping the people attain work throughout the whole country. We have monitored projects through completion through proper use of SDLC and donor relations. Unfortunately, all work and projects came to an end when the Taliban took control and we were evacuated due to security concerns. The last couple of months have been compiled with keeping up with the disaster of a situation and getting out individuals who are at high risk. I would like to use my experience and education to continue helping out with Refugees in America or where ever need be. My ability to speak Dari and Pashto and to understand the needs of the Refugees can be of use with your organizations goals. My Project Management background encompasses a focus in technology management, cyber technology, cloud security, and big data. My experience has allowed me to learn and grow to be successful in team building and project management from conception to completion. I excel in team motivation and management, and utilizing all available resources to achieve superior outcomes. During my career I have continually anticipated potential challenges and issues in order to proactively prepare for and resolve them. I am highly collaborative and believe that to be the foundation needed in any project in order to ensure total success.
